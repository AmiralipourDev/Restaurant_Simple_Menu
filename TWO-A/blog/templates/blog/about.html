History of our restaurant

The concept of eating out is ancient, but restaurants as we know them are fairly recent. In 18th century France, broth vendors like Boulanger offered "restorative" meals, coining the term "restaurant." These evolved into popular eateries, especially in Paris. By the 19th century, restaurants were a fixture of European social life.
